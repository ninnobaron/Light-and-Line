About me

My name is Nino Nguyen and I was born in Vietnam. I moved to Winnipeg at 17 and have lived here ever since. Since I was a kid, I have always loved art, but due to the education system which focuses entirely on science subjects, I could not do what I loved. Moving to Canada was a big decision for me and my family but I'm glad that I did because it turned out to be one of the best decisions I have ever made. Here, I can finally do what I love and make it my career.

I recently graduated from the University of Manitoba, my majors are graphic design and photography.
